About Us

At Baynur, we take pride in delivering top-tier specialised services tailored to meet your diverse needs.

Based in the heart of Aberdeen, Scotland, our team is committed to excellence, reliability, and meticulous attention to detail. Established on 11 December 2024, we are a private limited company dedicated to setting new standards across multiple industries. From cleaning and logistics to real estate management, we go above and beyond to ensure exceptional service delivery. Located at 56 Whitehouse Street, Aberdeen, AB10 1QH, we bring professionalism and precision to every job. Our goal is to exceed expectations and create value for our clients—because your satisfaction is our priority. Give us a chance and we promise you will be surprised by the quality of our services.
