About Us

At Baynur, we take pride in delivering top-tier specialised cleaning services tailored to meet your needs.

Based in the heart of Aberdeen, Scotland, our team is committed to excellence, reliability, and meticulous attention to detail. Established on 11 December 2024, we are a private limited company dedicated to setting new standards in the cleaning industry. Whether it’s deep cleaning, sanitation, or customised solutions for homes and businesses, we go above and beyond to ensure a spotless, refreshed environment. Located at 56 Whitehouse Street, Aberdeen, AB10 1QH, we bring professionalism and precision to every job. Our goal is to create cleaner, healthier spaces—because a clean environment is a happy one. Give us a chance and we promise you will be surprised by the quality of our services.

Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet your cleaning needs.

Office Cleaning

We provide expert office cleaning services to ensure a spotless and hygienic workspace. Our trained professionals use eco-friendly products for a healthier environment.

Carpet Cleaning

Revitalize your carpets with our deep-cleaning services! We remove dirt, stains, and allergens using eco-friendly products, leaving your carpets fresh, spotless, and looking like new.

End of Tenancy Cleaning

Ensure a hassle-free move with our thorough end-of-tenancy cleaning. We deep-clean every corner, leaving the property spotless and ready for the next tenant or final inspection.

Move-In Cleaning

Start fresh in your new home with our meticulous move-in cleaning service. We sanitize and deep-clean every space, ensuring a spotless and welcoming environment for you and your family.

Oven Cleaning

Say goodbye to grease and grime with our professional oven cleaning service. We remove stubborn stains and burnt residues, leaving your oven sparkling clean and ready for your next meal.

Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service goes beyond the surface, tackling hidden dirt, grime, and bacteria. From floors to ceilings, we leave every inch of your space fresh, sanitized, and looking its best.
