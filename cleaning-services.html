Professional Cleaning Services Available

At Baynur, we deliver exceptional cleaning services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to providing top-tier cleaning solutions for residential and commercial spaces throughout Aberdeen and the surrounding areas.

With our meticulous attention to detail and use of high-quality, eco-friendly products, we ensure a spotless, healthy environment for you, your family, or your business. Our flexible scheduling options and comprehensive service offerings make maintaining cleanliness easier than ever.

Residential Cleaning

Our residential cleaning services are designed to keep your home pristine and comfortable. Whether you need regular maintenance cleaning or a one-time deep clean, our team will exceed your expectations.

  • Regular home cleaning (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitization
  • Dusting and vacuuming all areas
  • Floor cleaning and polishing
  • Window cleaning and glass treatments

Commercial Cleaning

Maintain a professional, clean working environment with our commercial cleaning services. We understand the importance of a spotless workspace for productivity and brand image.

  • Office cleaning and maintenance
  • Retail space cleaning
  • Medical facility sanitization
  • Industrial space cleaning
  • Customized cleaning schedules

Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service addresses those hard-to-reach areas and stubborn dirt that regular cleaning might miss. Perfect for seasonal cleaning or preparing your space for special events.

  • Behind and under furniture cleaning
  • Detailed kitchen appliance cleaning
  • Cabinet and drawer interior cleaning
  • Light fixture and ceiling fan cleaning
  • Baseboard and door frame detailing

End of Tenancy Cleaning

Ensure the return of your security deposit with our thorough end of tenancy cleaning. We follow landlord and agency requirements to leave the property spotless.

  • Complete property sanitization
  • Oven and appliance deep cleaning
  • Carpet steam cleaning
  • Window and blind cleaning
  • Mold and mildew treatment

Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Revitalize your carpets and furniture with our specialized cleaning services. We remove stains, allergens, and odors to extend the life of your textiles.

  • Stain removal treatments
