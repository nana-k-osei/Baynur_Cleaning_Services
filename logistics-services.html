Comprehensive Cargo and Logistics Solutions

Baynur provides end-to-end cargo transport and logistics solutions across the UK and internationally, with specialist services to Nigeria and other African countries. Our experienced team ensures your goods move safely and efficiently through every stage of the supply chain.

Whether you need road freight services, international shipping, warehousing, or customs support, we offer reliable, cost-effective solutions tailored to your specific requirements. We understand the complexities of global logistics and are committed to delivering exceptional service on every shipment.

UK Road Freight

Our UK road freight services provide reliable transportation solutions throughout Great Britain and Northern Ireland, with flexible options to meet your delivery requirements.

  • Full Load (FTL) and Part Load (LTL) services
  • Expedited and same-day delivery options
  • Temperature-controlled transport
  • Hazardous goods handling
  • Nationwide coverage with competitive rates

International Shipping

Connect your business to global markets with our comprehensive international shipping services, offering both air and sea freight options to destinations worldwide.

  • Air freight for urgent deliveries
  • Sea freight for cost-effective bulk shipping
  • FCL and LCL container shipping
  • Multi-modal transport solutions
  • Cargo insurance and tracking services

African Logistics Solutions

With specialized expertise in African markets, particularly Nigeria, we offer tailored logistics solutions that navigate the unique challenges of shipping to and from the continent.

  • Specialist services to Nigeria and West Africa
  • East and South African logistics networks
  • Local partnerships for efficient delivery
  • Documentation and compliance expertise
  • Cultural understanding and market knowledge

Warehousing & Storage

Our flexible warehousing and storage solutions help you manage inventory efficiently, with secure facilities and comprehensive management services.

  • Short and long-term storage options
  • Inventory management systems
  • Pick and pack services
  • Cross-docking capabilities
  • Climate-controlled storage for sensitive goods

Customs Clearance

Navigate the complexities of international customs with our expert clearance services, ensuring smooth and compliant movement of goods across borders.

  • Import and export documentation preparation
  • Customs classification and valuation
  • Duty and tax calculation
  • Compliance with international trade regulations
  • Post-Brexit customs solutions

Last-Mile Delivery

Complete your supply chain with our efficient last-mile delivery services, ensuring your products reach their final destination on time and in perfect condition.

  • Urban and rural delivery networks
  • Time-window delivery options
  • Real-time tracking and notifications
  • Installation and assembly services
  • Returns management
