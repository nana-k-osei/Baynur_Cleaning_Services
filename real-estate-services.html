Professional Property Management & Short-Let Solutions

Baynur offers comprehensive property management services for landlords, investors, and property owners across Aberdeen and Scotland. We specialize in both traditional long-term rentals and the growing short-let market, helping you maximize returns while minimizing hassle.

Our experienced team takes a personalized approach to property management, offering tailored solutions based on your specific needs and goals. Whether you're looking to optimize a single investment property or manage a diverse portfolio, we provide end-to-end services from tenant finding and property maintenance to full Airbnb management.

Property Management

Our full-service property management takes the stress out of being a landlord, handling every aspect of your property rental to ensure smooth operations and maximum returns.

  • Comprehensive tenant management
  • Rent collection and financial reporting
  • Regular property inspections
  • Legal compliance and documentation
  • 24/7 tenant support and emergency response

Short-Term Lets & Airbnb Management

Maximize your property's income potential with our full-service short-term let and Airbnb management, handling everything from listing creation to guest communications.

  • Professional listing creation with photography
  • Dynamic pricing optimization
  • Guest screening and communication
  • Cleaning and turnover management
  • 24/7 guest support and key handling

Tenant Finding & Lettings

Find reliable, quality tenants for your property with our comprehensive tenant finding service, ensuring your rental is occupied by responsible individuals.

  • Market analysis and rental valuation
  • Professional marketing and advertising
  • Tenant referencing and credit checks
  • Lease preparation and signing
  • Inventory and property condition reports

Property Sourcing & Acquisitions

Expand your property portfolio with our expert sourcing service, identifying high-potential investments that align with your financial goals.

Property Maintenance
